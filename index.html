Essay

Why we keep returning to the dark room.

Cinema is a peculiar medium. It asks you to sit still in the dark with strangers and trust that the next two hours will be worth it. The good ones reward that trust; the great ones reshape it. This collection leans toward the latter.

Each entry was chosen for a single reason: it changed how its director, or its audience, thought about what film could do. Some are quietly formal, others are loud and operatic. Together they form a personal, defensible canon.

Cinema is a matter of what's in the frame and what's out.

Martin Scorsese

What this canon values

  1. A clear point of view from the director.
  2. Performances that age into something deeper than charm.
  3. An ending that earns the time you gave it.

What it deliberately ignores

  • Box office position in opening weekend.
  • Number of sequels or franchise potential.
  • Whether it is currently fashionable to like.

Glossary

Mise en scène
Everything intentionally arranged inside a single frame: actors, props, light, color.
Diegetic sound
Audio whose source exists inside the world of the film, such as a radio a character switches on.
DoP
The crew member responsible for the camera and lighting departments.
